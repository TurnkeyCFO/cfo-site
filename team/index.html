How to use this portal

  • Pick a section above. CFO SOPs and Role Manuals cover process and training; Turnkey Web and the Dialer Playbook cover delivery and outbound.
  • Any checklist item you tick is saved in your own browser, so you can pick up where you left off.
  • Role manuals show a progress bar so you can see how far through training you are.
  • Client Dossiers sit behind a team password. They are internal reference only, never for client distribution.
