Dialer Playbook

Cold-call scripts, dispositions, and objection handling for the dialer team.
8 pages

Everything a Turnkey Web cold-call rep needs: who we hire and how the 1099 contractor relationship works, the onboarding checklist from signed agreement to first solo session, the daily three-campaign workflow, the call scripts and objection responses, the nine disposition tags that drive the whole routing machine, and the outbound calling compliance rules (do-not-call, calling hours, recording consent) every rep acknowledges before dialing. CloudTalk is the phone, HubSpot is the brain, and one correct tag per call is the only data-entry duty. The tag names, follow-up intervals, and campaign structure are locked; scripts and timing flex. Start with the daily workflow, then dispositions, then the scripts. Booking a meeting lives in the Turnkey Web section.
