Turnkey Web SOPs

Delivery SOPs for website builds, previews, onboarding, and handoff.
5 pages

How Turnkey Web turns a lead into a live site: booking a discovery meeting on a live call, taking an intake through the automated build, reviewing and approving the preview, capturing payment on the 50/50 build split, and servicing the monthly care plan after go-live. Websites are the product and credibility is the pitch. Nothing reaches a prospect without a named approval, and no money moves without Ricky's sign-off. Start with intake to build, then preview and payment, then the care plan. The dialer playbook feeds this funnel, and the CFO communication standards govern the voice on anything client-facing.
