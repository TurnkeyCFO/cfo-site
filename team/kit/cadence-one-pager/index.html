Your monthly rhythm

Every month follows the same path. We work in business days (BD). BD1 is the first business day after your prior month ends. Here is exactly what happens, and what you receive, at each step.

BD1Open

We open the month

Bank and card activity flows in and the prior month is queued to close. Nothing needed from you.

BD3You

Open items list to you

One short list of anything we need: a statement, a receipt or memo for a few transactions, an approval. Quick replies keep your close on time.

BD6Review

Full quality review

Your books get a complete review for accuracy before anything goes out.

BD10Delivered

Financials in your hands

Your closed month is delivered with a short plain language summary of what changed and what to look at.

What you receive, and when

WhenWhat lands in your inbox
Around BD3Your open items list, the few things we need from you this month
By BD10Profit and loss, balance sheet, and a short summary note
On your scheduleYour monthly review conversation, if your plan includes one

The short version: reply to the open items list by BD3, and your financials are in your hands by BD10. Same rhythm, every month.
