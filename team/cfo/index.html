Turnkey CFO SOPs

Process SOPs for bookkeeping, monthly close, payroll, AP/AR, and reporting.
19 pages

These are the operating SOPs for the bookkeeping side of the firm: how a client is onboarded, how the monthly close runs on the BD1, BD3, BD6, BD10, BD12 rhythm, how payroll and bill pay are handled, how we communicate, which platforms we touch and how, and what gets escalated to Ricky. Turnkey CFO is a bookkeeping firm. Read the monthly close SOP first, then onboarding, then the rest as your work touches them. Each SOP is the law for its area, and the role manuals point you to them. When something is unclear, ask rather than guess.
