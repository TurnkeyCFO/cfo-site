Payroll Float & Cash Timing

You pay contractors weekly. Clients pay net-30 (or 45, or 60). That gap can choke growth or create a liquidity crisis when volume spikes. Most firms don't model this properly.

Margin by Placement Type

Temp, contract, direct hire — each has a different margin profile. Without placement-level reporting, you can't tell which revenue is actually building your business.

Client Concentration Risk

When one client is 40% of your revenue, that's not just a business risk — it's a financial one. Lenders and acquirers want to see a diversified book.
