Why employers switch

Process

Structured search assignments, clear communication, stronger outcomes.

Vida emphasizes a process-oriented approach, with each search tailored to the function, vertical, and location while following a repeatable framework.

1

Align on the role and hiring context

Define the business need, success profile, culture fit, and urgency behind the search.

2

Activate targeted outreach channels

Leverage established networks and search pathways that may sit beyond an internal TA team’s reach.

3

Present an initial candidate grouping

Move toward a first slate within 2 to 3 weeks, keeping decision-makers close to the process.

4

Support interview, offer, and onboarding readiness

Help both sides stay informed and prepared through interviews, negotiation, and start-date transition.
