What does this cost? +
Pricing depends on the complexity of your entity structure and reporting needs. We typically quote monthly retainers from $2,000 to $5,000+. Get an instant estimate based on your deal flow and entity count.
Do you work with investors or just brokerages? +
Both. We work with real estate investors managing multi-property portfolios, brokers running teams and managing commission splits, and everything in between. Deal-level and entity-level reporting, whatever fits your structure.
Can you help with our entity structure? +
We don't do tax filings, but we work alongside your CPA to make sure your structure serves your financial reporting and growth strategy. We advise on reporting needs, consolidation opportunities, and entity efficiency.
How fast can we start? +
Typical onboarding is 2 weeks. We get QuickBooks access, map your entities, pull historical data, and deliver your first consolidated close within that window. From there, we're live on a 15th/EOM close schedule.
