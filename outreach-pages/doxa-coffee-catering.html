Common Questions

Questions We Get a Lot

What do you need to set up?

Just a 6x6-foot space for the cart, one standard power outlet (15–20 amp dedicated circuit), and wheelchair-accessible entry. If your event is outdoors or lacks power, we can bring a generator — just let us know ahead of time.

What types of events do you cater?

All of them — weddings, corporate events, private parties, birthdays, showers, festivals, film sets, community events, and more. If you have a gathering, we can caffeinate it. Just reach out and we'll let you know what we can do.

Do you only serve Austin?

We're based in Austin and primarily serve the Greater Austin area, including Dripping Springs, the Hill Country, Round Rock, Georgetown, San Marcos, and down to San Antonio. If your event is outside that area, reach out — we're happy to provide a custom quote for travel.

Can you serve outdoors?

Absolutely. Our cart is fully mobile and perfect for outdoor events. If power isn't available on-site, we bring a generator. Whether it's a backyard brunch, an outdoor wedding, or an open-air market, we've got you covered.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend reaching out at least 3–4 weeks before your event, though we've worked with shorter timelines when needed. Peak wedding season (spring and fall) fills up fast — the earlier the better.
