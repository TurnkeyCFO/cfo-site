Frequently asked

What does this cost? ▼
We provide an instant estimate based on your agency structure, revenue, and current financial visibility. Most agencies invest between $1,500–$3,500/month depending on complexity. No setup fees, no long-term contracts. You can start with a 3-month pilot and scale from there.
We already have a bookkeeper — do we need this? ▼
A bookkeeper records what happened. TurnkeyCFO tells you what it means — per-client margin, pricing strategy, and whether your cost structure actually supports your growth. Most of our clients keep their bookkeeper and add us as the strategic layer on top.
How do you handle retainer vs. project revenue? ▼
We build the recognition schedules and reporting structure to handle both accurately, regardless of how your billing is structured. Many agencies mix retainer and project work. We create clean separation so you can see profitability by revenue model.
How fast to get started? ▼
Typically 2 weeks. Week one is a 15-minute call plus document gathering. Week two we deliver your baseline P&L, preliminary per-client profitability, and a roadmap. Then we transition into ongoing monthly engagements.
