Common bookkeeping questions answered.

Straight answers to what small business owners and nonprofit teams actually want to know before hiring a bookkeeper.

Do you offer bookkeeping near me, or is this fully remote?

TurnkeyCFO is based in Austin, TX and works with clients both locally and nationwide. Our service is fully virtual — you get the same clean books, live dashboards, and 24-hour response time whether you're in Austin or running a business across the country.

How much does monthly bookkeeping cost for a small business?

Pricing depends on your transaction volume, number of accounts, and which services you need (bookkeeping only vs. bookkeeping + payroll + 1099). Use the Instant Estimate tool on this page to get a real price range in about 60 seconds — no call required.

What's included in your monthly bookkeeping services?

Every engagement includes accurate transaction categorization, bank and credit card reconciliations, a full monthly close package (P&L, balance sheet, cash flow statement), and written commentary on what moved and why. Most clients also add payroll, 1099 management, AP/AR, and a live financial dashboard.

What's the difference between a bookkeeper and a CPA?

A bookkeeper handles day-to-day financial recordkeeping — categorizing transactions, reconciling accounts, running payroll, and producing monthly financial statements. A CPA handles tax preparation, tax strategy, and filing. TurnkeyCFO does the bookkeeping and coordinates with your CPA for tax filings — keeping your books filing-ready year-round.

Can you clean up messy or behind-on books?

Yes — catch-up bookkeeping is one of the most common first projects. We've rebuilt books three years behind, untangled multi-entity messes, and cleaned up misclassified transactions going back to day one. We scope the cleanup, give you a clear estimate, and get you current before setting up the ongoing monthly workflow.

Do you work with nonprofits and churches?

Yes. TurnkeyCFO works with nonprofits, churches, and faith-based organizations — including fund accounting, donor-restricted fund tracking, clergy housing allowance payroll, and 1099s for guest speakers. We have a dedicated church bookkeeping page with full detail.

How quickly can we get started?

Most clients are fully onboarded within 1–2 weeks. 15-minute intro call → we connect to your accounts → clean historical data → monthly close and dashboard live. No long ramp-up, no month-long onboarding projects.

Do you handle payroll and 1099s, or just the books?

Both. TurnkeyCFO handles payroll processing (through Gusto or your existing provider), contractor 1099 management (W-9 collection, threshold tracking, year-end filing), accounts payable, and accounts receivable. Most clients use us as their complete back-office finance team.
