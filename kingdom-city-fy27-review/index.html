03 — Sources of income

Three streams, one shifting toward home.

Kingdom City is funded by member giving plus a schedule of partner churches, with the Vision Fund's designated giving on top. The plan intentionally lets partner support decline ~25%/yr as internal giving ramps from $45 to $90 per attendee — Blake's stated goal, reached by July 2027.

04 — Vision Fund
Designated giving

The Vision Fund is real giving — counted in total revenue, tracked on its own line.

A designated stream of about $39,710 for FY27 — roughly 55 committed donors at $76/month, ramping to its full $4,180/month run-rate by December 2026. It carries its own line so leadership can always see it distinctly, while it counts toward the church's total giving.
