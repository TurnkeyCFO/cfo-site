Bill pay & expenses move to Ramp — starting August

Once the books are fully on us, your accounts payable and card spend consolidate onto Ramp, which removes the Bill.com and Expensify fees and feeds straight into your books.

Today your bills run through Bill.com and your card spend through Expensify, two separate subscriptions. Beginning in August, we replace both with Ramp, which is free to you, handles bill pay and card controls in one place, and syncs directly into QuickBooks. We migrate your vendors and recurring payments over so nothing is missed, then wind both tools down. Your approval still gates every payment, so you stay in control of what goes out and when.

Your transition timeline

A clean, staged handoff: payroll moves to us first in July, then a full books migration in August, with a real cleanup of the back history.

No coverage gap during the handoff: your current bookkeeper runs the June books in July, closing out June as they normally would. We then fully migrate the books to Turnkey in August. That brief, intentional overlap means nothing slips through the cracks. Your first close fully on us is August's books, delivered in early September.
Books, Reporting & Migration
  • First (this week): We set up your client portal and deliver your engagement letter and payment request. You e-sign and set up payment, then we begin.
  • Late June – July: Accept access to your systems (QuickBooks, bank view-only, Bill.com, Expensify, Planning Center, GivFlow). We verify the books and feeds and hold your in-person onboarding meeting.
  • July: Your current bookkeeper closes June as normal. We run in parallel, get to know the books, and take over the July 30 payroll run.
  • August (full migration): The books move fully to Turnkey. We begin the cleanup, chart-of-accounts rebuild, and proper fund accounting, and we start the Ramp migration (AP + expenses off Bill.com and Expensify).
  • First close (early September, for August): Your first full monthly close on us, live dashboard, and a board-ready summary, delivered ahead of your board meeting.
